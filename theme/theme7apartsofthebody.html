Malagasy–Tsimihety Theme Lesson 7a: Parts of the Body

(Malagasy–Tsimihety with Bako — Thematic Lesson 7a) • January 2025

Ny Vatan'ny Olona

Red = Tsimihety  |  Blue = Official Malagasy

1. Key Vocabulary

Ny Loha (lohaoff / lôhatsm) — The head

Ny Vatana — The body

Ny Rantsam-Batana — The limbs

2. Learning the Vocabulary for Body Parts

1. Listen repeatedly

Example: maso (eye)

2. Listen and point to the part on your body

Example: maso

3. Point and say: "ITY" (this) or "IRETO" (these)

In response to the question: "AIZA ny …?" (Where is the …?)

Aiza ny maso? → Response: ITY or IRETO

4. Listen and repeat twice

maso >> maso, maso

5. Answer the questions: "INONA ity / ireto?" (What is this / What are these?)

Inona ity? → Response: MASO (eye)
