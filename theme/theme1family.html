Ny Fianakaviana — The Family

Family size tends to be large in Madagascar. Relationships between family members are important in daily life, through good and bad times.

  • Starting a family is one of the main purposes of marriage — it is challenging being single or childless in the Malagasy culture.
  • Malagasies consider uncles/aunties like parents, cousins like siblings, and nephews/nieces like one's own children.
  • Marriage is a family affair, not just between the couple.
  • Specific terms for family members are very complicated in Malagasy, and Tsimihety is even more complicated than official Malagasy.
