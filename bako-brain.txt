## CORE RULES (brief)
- Sentence order: PREDICATE first, subject last. "Miasa izy" not "Izy miasa."
- Tense: M-=present, N-=past, H-=future. Miasa/Niasa/Hiasa.
- Negation: TSY before verb. Tsy miasa izy.
- Definite article: NY. Ve/Moa = yes/no question particle.

## TSIMIHETY (tsm) vs OFFICIAL (off) — KEY DIFFERENCES
Pronouns: zaho(I), anao(you), izy(he/she), antsika(we incl), zahay/aminay(we excl), areo(you pl), zareo(they)
Off equivalents: aho, ianao, izy, isika, izahay, ianareo, izy ireo

Question words (tsm→off): Manino(Maninona/what doing), Ino(Inona/what), Zôvy(Iza/who), Tafiriagna(Oviana/when past), Hafiriagna(Rahoviana/when future), Manakôry(Manahoana/how), Ohatrino(Ohatrinona/how much), Njôvy/Anjôvy(N'iza/whose), Ho anjôvy(Ho an'iza/for whom), Isaka ino(Isaky ny inona/how often)

Yes/No: Ia(Eny=yes), Ehe(Tsia=no)

Key dialect vocab (tsm→off→English):
- Tragno(Trano=house), Lakôro(Fefy=fence), Tamiagna(Varavarana=door), Rimba(Rindrina=wall)
- Hely(Kely=small), Geda(Lehibe=big), Ambo(Avo=high), Mahety(Tery=cramped)
- Mahamay(Mafana=hot), Manintsy(Mangatsiaka=cold), Ôragnandro(Orana=rain), Masôva(Masoandro=sun), Vôndro(Zavona=fog), Rôndro(Rahona=cloud), Rivotro(Rivotra=wind), Langitry(Lanitra=sky)
- Homagna(Misakafo=eat a meal), Miseky(Mandro=shower), Mionjogno(Mifoha=wake up), Mandry(Matory=sleep), Mimpôdy(Mody=go home), Magnala disaka(Miala sasatra=rest), Magnomagna(Miomana=prepare)
- Maraindraigny(Maraina=morning), Matsagna(Atoandro=midday), Harivariva(Hariva=evening), Aligny(Alina=night)
- Misoma(Milalao=play), Migiaka(Misotro=drink / Mihinana=eat)
- Mijôtso(Midina=go down), Magnavaratra(Mianavaratra=go north), Magnatsimo(Mianatsimo=go south)
- Marigny(Akaiky=near), Lavitry(Lavitra=far), Aketo/Akato/Akeo/Akao/Akany(locative pronouns here/there/far)
- Vôlagna(Volana=month), Taogno(Taona=year), Nomoaligny(Omaly=yesterday), Niagny(Androany=today), Amaraigny(Rahampitso=tomorrow)
- Sôfigny(Sofina=ears), Ôrogno(Orona=nose), Hongotro(Tongotra=feet), Bôko(Kibo=stomach), Tahezagna(Lamosina=back), Sôra(Tarehy=face), Havay(Soroka=shoulders), Maramaragna(Volo=hair)
- Zama(uncle maternal), Iadabe/Iadahely(uncle paternal)
- Satrià(Satria=because), Avake(Avy eo=then), Avite(Dia=towards), Ndraiky(Sy=and), Ndreky(sy connecting nouns), Samby(Samy=both), Sandrô(Sao/Fandrao=in case/lest), Tsotsoko(Soa ihany fa=fortunately), Mahazany(Noho izany=therefore), Izikoa(Raha/Rehefa=when/if), Beky(Mba h-=so that)
- Lera(Adiny=hour of travel). Làlagn(Làlana=road). Làlagn 3 lera = 3 hours travel.
- Kambo(Kisoa=pig), Anambe(Anana=green leaves), Tomaty(Voatabia=tomatoes), Tongolo lay(Tongolo gasy=garlic), Sakaitany(Sakamalao=ginger), Tongolo be(Tongolo ejipta=onion)

Jobs prefix: Mp- = person who does something habitually. Mpanjaitra(tailor), Mpandrafitra(carpenter), Mpamboly(farmer), Mpiompy(animal breeder), Mpitsabo(nurse), Mpanamboatra fiara(mechanic).

Indirect object: HO + person. Structure: Verb-HO+person-Subject. Ho ahy(for me), ho anao(for you), ho azy(for him/her), ho antsika(for us incl), ho aminay(for us excl), ho anareo(for you pl), ho aminjareo(for them). Question: Ho anjôvy?(tsm) / Ho an'iza?(off)

Passive voice pairs: Manasa/Sasana, Manadio/Diovina, Mahandro/Andrahoina, Mitetika/Tetehana, Mividy/Vidiana, Manao/Atao, Mamaky/Vakina, Mila/Ilaina, Manisy/Asiana, Mandroso/Aroso.

Possessives: NOM+suffix. Mofo+ZAHAY=MofoNAY. Question: NOM-ZÔVY?(tsm) / NOM-N'IZA?(off)

Demonstratives (tsm): tôho(near/ity off), io(intermediate), rôho(farther/itsy off), iny(very far)

Comparisons: Toa an'/Toy ny/Mitovy amin'(equal). Kokoa noho(more than). Duplication=less extreme: tsaratsara, mafanafana, mangatsiatsiaka. Letter changes in duplication: l→d, v→b, s→ts, r→dr.

Amin'ny(with/using instrument). Past: tamin'ny. Question: Amin'ny ino?(tsm)

Time: Amin'ny(present/future)→Tamin'ny(past). This week(tsm): herinandro tôho. Last: herinandro teo igny. Next: herinandro io/rôho.

Church: Fiderana(worship), Fanatitra(offering), Toriteny(sermon), Tsodrano(benediction), Vakiteny(Bible reading). ANTSIKA=inclusive we, used in church.

Cultural: HAODY=knock equivalent. TOMPOKO=polite address. AZAFADY=please/excuse me. Houses face west. Zebu=wealth/ceremonies. Rice 3x daily. 2 seasons: hot/wet(Oct-Mar), cool/dry. Mandritsara=Sofia region.

Directions: Mivily ankavia(left), Mivily ankavanana(right), Mandeha mahitsy(straight), Miakatra(up), Mijôtso/Midina(down), Miampita(cross), Mandalo(pass by), Mihodina(turn around). N=Avaratra, S=Atsimo, W=Andrefagna, E=Atinianagna.
