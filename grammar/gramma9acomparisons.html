Malagasy–Tsimihety Grammar Lesson 9a

Comparisons

February 2025 — Malagasy–Tsimihety with Bako

COMPARISON (FAMPITAHANA)

A – Equality

The following comparison markers can be used interchangeably to express similarity:

  • Toa an’
  • Tahaka an’ / ny
  • Toy ny
  • Mitovy amin’
  • Sahala amin’

These correspond to English expressions such as “as … as” or “… like …”.

Predicate
(Adjective, Noun, or Verb)		 Comparison Marker Subject Personal Pronoun Object Personal Pronoun
Nosy (island) toa an’ / tahaka an’
(like)		 I Angletera I Madagasikara
Mamy (sweet) mitovy amin’ / sahala amin’
(as … as)		 ny siramamy (sugar) ny tantely (honey)

Example Sentences:

Nosy toa an’I Madagasikara I Angletera.
→ England is an island like Madagascar.

Mamy mitovy amin’ny tantely ny siramamy.
→ Sugar is as sweet as honey.

B – Both (SAMY in Official Malagasy, SAMBY in Tsimihety)

The following markers indicate that two elements are similar or equivalent:

  • Samy (Off) / Samby (Tsm) – Used before a predicate to indicate both subjects share the same quality.
  • Sy (Off) / Ndreky (Tsm) – Used to connect two nouns indicating both belong together.
Comparison Marker Predicate
(Adjective, Noun, or Verb)		 Name/Noun + Sy / Ndreky Name/Noun
Samy / Samby
(both)		 Nosy (island) I Madagasikara sy I Angletera
Samy / Samby
(both)		 Mamy (sweet) Ny siramamy ndreky Ny fandrama (honey)

Example Sentences:

Samy mamy ny tantely sy ny siramamy.
→ Both sugar and honey are sweet.

Samy nosy I Madagasikara sy I Angletera.
→ Both Madagascar and England are islands.

1. MAMY toy ny akondro ny voasary
2. MAMY tahaka ny akondro ny voasary
3. MAMY sahala amin’ny akondro ny voasary
4. Samy MAMY ny akondro sy ny voasary
(Tsm) Samby MAMY ny fontsy ndreky tsoà

C – Exercise

Create four sentences like the examples above using these words:

I Madagasikara (Madagascar) – I Angletera (England) → TSARA TAREHY (beautiful)

Akoho (chicken) – hena omby (beef) → MATSIRO (delicious)

Translate the following sentences into your native language:

  1. Samby mazava ny masôva ndreky davôlana.
  2. Samby mangitry ny manasy ndreky manga.
  3. Samby tsara amin’ny fahasalamana ny legioma ndreky voankazo.
  4. Samby nianatra malagasy zahay.
  5. Mangatsiaka mitovy amin’ I Suisse I Canada.
  6. Lafo toy ny akoho ny hena omby.
  7. Dokotera tahaka ny papany izy.
  8. Lava toa azy ny zanany.
