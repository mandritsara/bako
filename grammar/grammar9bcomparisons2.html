Malagasy–Tsimihety Grammar Lesson 9b

Comparisons (II)

March 2025 — Malagasy–Tsimihety with Bako

COMPARISON (FAMPITAHANA)

Kokoa noho

(Used for comparisons)
Equivalent to “more than” or “-er than”.

There are 2 ways of constructing a sentence with this:

Structure 1

Predicate Kokoa noho Object Subject
Mamy (sweet) Kokoa noho ny siramamy (sugar) ny tantely (honey)

Structure 2

Predicate Kokoa Subject Noho
Mamy (sweet) Kokoa ny tantely (honey) Noho ny siramamy (sugar)

Mamy ny tantely.
Mamy koa ny siramamy.
FA
Mamy KOKOA NOHO ny siramamy ny tantely.
Mamy KOKOA ny tantely NOHO ny siramamy.
> Honey is sweeter than sugar.

Practice Exercises

Comparing age

ANTITRA / ANTITRYtsm = old

Example:

I Soa – I Vao
Antitra kokoa noho i Soa i Vao.
Antitra kokoa i Vao noho i Soa.

→ Vao is older than Soa.
