Malagasy-Tsimihety with Bako: Lesson 1b - Goodbye

Common Ways to Say Goodbye

There are a number of ways to say goodbye in Malagasy. We will start with the most common ones:

  • Veloma tompoko
  • Veloma e
  • Mazotoa

Additional Info

“Indray” = “koa” , and can be used interchangeably.

To specify a time when you will meet again, add “again/koa” at the end.

Meeting Again at a Specific Time

  • Veloma/Mazotoa + harivariva koa (see you in the afternoon)
  • Veloma/Mazotoa + rahampitso off / amarainy tsm koa (see you tomorrow)
  • Veloma/Mazotoa + Alarobia koa (see you on Wednesday)

If you want to be brief, you can drop “veloma/mazotoa” and simply say the time when you will meet again followed by “koa”. For example:

  • Amin’ny alina koa (see you tonight)
  • Amarainy koa (see you tomorrow)
  • Talata koa (see you on Tuesday)

Don’t forget to add the word “koa” at the end of the sentence!

To say “see you later”, you can say REHEFA AVY EO (in a short while — minutes to hours on the same day) or MANDRAPIHAONA (on the same day or the next).

Goodbye Before Eating

Person 1: Mazotoa homana tsm / mihinana off

Person 2: Misaotra ianao koa


Vocabulary

  • Veloma = goodbye
  • Manaraka = next
  • Indray = again
  • Koa = again
  • Homana tsm / mihinana off = to eat

Appendix: Days, Weeks, and Months

Parts of the Day

  • Morning: Maraina off / Maraindrainy tsm
  • Noon: Atoandro off / Matsagna tsm
  • Afternoon: Hariva off / Harivariva tsm
  • Night: Alina

Days of the Week

  • Monday: Alatsinainy off / Tinainy tsm
  • Tuesday: Talata
  • Wednesday: Alarobia off / Robia tsm
  • Thursday: Alakamisy off / Kamisy tsm
  • Friday: Zoma
  • Saturday: Asabotsy off / Sabotsy tsm
  • Sunday: Alahady off / Lahady-Dimansy tsm

Months of the Year

Both English and French influenced words exist in Malagasy. Tsimihety prefers the French versions.

  • January: Janoary off / Zanvie tsm
  • February: Febroary off / Fevrie tsm
  • March: Martsa off / Marsa tsm
  • April: Aprily off / Avrily tsm
  • May: May
  • June: Jona
  • July: Jolay
  • August: Aogositra
  • September: Septambra
  • October: Oktobra
  • November: Novambra
  • December: Desambra
