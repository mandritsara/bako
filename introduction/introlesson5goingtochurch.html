Arriving, greeting, asking service times, and simple church vocabulary.

Arriving & Greeting

Greeter: Tonga soa eto amin’ny fiangonana. — Welcome to church.
You: Misaotra betsaka. Faly tonga eto izahay. — Thank you very much. We’re glad to be here.
Greeter: Inona ny anaranaooff? / Anaranao manahogytsm? — What’s your name?
You: … no anarakooff / Anarako …tsm.

Polite add‑ons

  • Azafady — please / excuse me
  • Tsisy fisaorana — you’re welcome
  • Misaotra betsaka — thanks very much

Service Times & Location

You: Rahoviana ny fotoam‑pivavahana? — When is the service?
Greeter: Alahady maraina amin’ny 8:00. — Sunday morning at 8am.
You: Aiza ny fiangonana? — Where is the church?
Greeter: Eto akaiky / Any amin’ny …(place)….

Time words

  • Alahady — Sunday
  • maraina — morning
  • hariva — afternoon/evening
  • androany — today; rahampitsooff / amarainytsm — tomorrow

Participating in the Service

Leader: Hanomboka isika amin’ny vavaka. — We will start with prayer.
Leader: Hira fiderana aloha, avy eo toriteny. — Hymns first, then the sermon.
You: Afaka mangataka vavaka ve? — Can I request prayer?

Helpful phrases

  • Hivavaka ho anao izahay. — We will pray for you.
  • Misaotra tamin’ny fandraisana. — Thank you for welcoming us.

In informal settings, people often say araka izay vitantsika — as we’re able.
